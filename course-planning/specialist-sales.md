# Online Sales & Conversion Specialist — Analysis & Recommendations

---

## 1. PRICING STRATEGY

**EFT/TFT Professional Training at $1,370**

Comparable EFT practitioner programs run $1,200-$3,500 for online formats. However, $1,370 feels arbitrary.

**Recommendation:** Round to $1,397. The "97" ending is well-tested. It sits just below the $1,400 psychological barrier. $1,497 is also defensible after social proof is built up.

**Rebirth of the Soul at $1,290**

This is aggressive for a self-improvement course targeting a general consumer audience. Self-esteem courses typically range $197-$797. Only Brene Brown-tier names command $1,000+.

**Recommendation:** Drop to $497 or $697. Not a reflection of value but of market positioning. Reserve higher pricing for a future "premium" tier with group coaching calls.

**The $20 Manual PDF**

Underpriced to function as a product, overpriced to function as a lead magnet. At $20, generates negligible revenue and doesn't build an email list.

**Recommendation:**
- **Option A (Lead Magnet):** Make it free for an email address. Build a list, nurture toward Course 2.
- **Option B (Tripwire):** Price at $7-$9 as a tripwire. Strip to first 2-3 chapters, not the full book. "Continue your journey with the full course" CTA.

Do not sell the full book content for $20 alongside a $497+ course of the same material. It is cannibalization.

## 2. VALUE PERCEPTION

**Key value elements currently missing:**

- **Outcome specificity.** "Be qualified to offer EFT sessions to your clients within 5 weeks."
- **Credential authority.** Affiliation with AAMET, EFT International, or similar. Or Renata's own credentials doing the heavy lifting.
- **Income potential framing.** "EFT practitioners charge $80-$200 per session. This course pays for itself after 7-17 client sessions."
- **Curriculum breakdown.** Not "Week 1: Introduction" but "Week 1: The Science of Tapping — Understanding the neuroscience behind why EFT works (so you can explain it confidently to skeptical clients)."

For Course 2, value perception shifts from ROI to transformation narrative. Before/after emotional states, not feature lists.

---

## 3. BUNDLE PSYCHOLOGY

20% is reasonable. But the presentation matters more than the percentage.

**Recommendation:** Do not lead with the bundle. Lead with the EFT course. After purchase or during checkout, offer Course 2 as a discounted add-on ("order bump"):

> "Add Rebirth of the Soul for just $297 — a special price exclusively for EFT students. This program deepens your own emotional foundation, making you a more effective practitioner."

If presenting a bundle on the sales page, use the "savings" frame, not the "discount" frame. Show combined value, then bundle price, then savings amount.

## 4. THE $20 PDF (Expanded)

**The strongest play:** Make the first 2-3 chapters available as a free PDF in exchange for name and email. This feeds into an automated email sequence (5-7 emails over 2 weeks):

1. Welcome + PDF delivery
2. "Why self-esteem is a neuroscience problem, not a willpower problem"
3. A short exercise from the course (delivers value)
4. Testimonial or transformation story
5. "The full journey" — introduces Course 2
6. Objection handling (FAQ style)
7. Final CTA with urgency

---

## 5. LANDING PAGE STRUCTURE

**For the EFT Professional Training, the sales page should follow this sequence:**

1. **Hero Section** — "Become a Certified EFT/TFT Practitioner in 5 Weeks — and Start Helping Clients Transform Their Lives." CTA visible above the fold.
2. **The Problem** — Pain points of target audience.
3. **The Solution** — Course as bridge. 3-4 sentences.
4. **Instructor Credentials** — Photo, bio, psychology degree, neuroscience research, years of practice, affiliations.
5. **Curriculum Breakdown** — Week-by-week, expandable/collapsible.
6. **What You Get** — Bullet list of everything included.
7. **Social Proof** — Testimonials, logos, numbers.
8. **ROI Calculator** — "This training pays for itself after your first 10-15 client sessions."
9. **FAQ Section** — Top 5-7 objections.
10. **Guarantee** — 14-day satisfaction guarantee.
11. **Final CTA** — Repeat offer + "Have questions?" link.
12. **Sticky CTA** — Persistent button on scroll.

**For Course 2,** tone shifts to emotional/aspirational. "Rebuild Your Self-Worth from the Inside Out." Transformation stories replace ROI calculations.

## 6. SOCIAL PROOF

**What is needed:**

- **Video testimonials** (2-3 clips, 60-90 seconds). 3-5x more persuasive than text at this price.
- **Specific outcome testimonials.** Not "Renata is wonderful" but "After completing the training, I added EFT sessions to my practice and booked 8 new clients."
- **Professional credentials display.** University affiliations, research publications, conference presentations.
- **Numbers.** Years of experience, number of clients served, number of students trained.
- **Media or press mentions.**

If video testimonials don't exist yet, this should be Renata's immediate priority.

---

## 7. URGENCY & SCARCITY

**Recommendation: Hybrid model — rolling enrollment with periodic cohort bonuses.**

- Course available for purchase at any time (evergreen).
- Every quarter, offer a "live cohort bonus" — purchasers get a live Q&A, extra bonus, or small-group session.
- Countdown timer during enrollment windows (only if the deadline is real).

For Course 2, an introductory launch price ("$497 for the first 50 students, then $697") is effective.

## 8. CROSS-SELL & UPSELL STRATEGY

1. **Lead magnet** (free PDF) → email sequence → **Course 2** ($497)
2. **Course 2 buyers** → upsell to **EFT Training** ($1,397)
3. **EFT Training buyers** → order bump **Course 2** at $297
4. **All course buyers** → upsell to **1-on-1 coaching** ($350/session)
5. **EFT graduates** → "advanced practitioner" or "supervision" package (future product)

Key insight: the $350 coaching session is the highest-margin product. Every course should funnel toward coaching as the premium tier.

## 9. TRUST BUILDING FOR INTERNATIONAL AUDIENCE

- **Language quality.** All English copy must be flawless. Have a native speaker review.
- **International credentials.** Emphasize internationally recognized training/certifications.
- **Stripe payment.** Show "Secure payment" indicator near CTAs.
- **Currency.** Price in USD (already correct).
- **Testimonials from English-speaking clients.** Even one carries outsized weight.
- **Professional photo and video.** High-quality visuals signal professionalism.
- **Clear communication channels.** WhatsApp + visible email.
- **"About me" video.** Hearing the instructor speak builds trust faster than any text.

## 10. CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION

**CTA Language:**
- EFT: "Start Your EFT Certification" or "Begin Your Practitioner Training"
- Course 2: "Start Your Transformation" or "Begin the Program"
- Bundle: "Get Both Programs & Save"

For Course 2:

- "I've tried other programs and nothing worked"

## 11. THE FREE GIFT STRATEGY

**Do:**
- Position as bonus, not core component. "Receive our complete EFT/TFT Practitioner Manual ($47 value) as a bonus."
- Frame as reference tool post-course: "Your go-to reference guide for your first 100 EFT sessions."
- List in "What You Get" as a separate line item with perceived value.

**Do not:**
- Make it the primary selling point.
- Confuse it with Course 2 content. Keep naming and positioning distinct.
